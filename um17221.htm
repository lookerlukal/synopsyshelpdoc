17.2.2.1 Dummy custom coatings                                                Next Previous TOC

 

In the event that the coating design is not known, it is possible to model only the effects. This a simplified model, with no angular or wavelength sensitivity.  It is defined by the user in FILM with a stack of the form (see section 16.1)

 

RFILM

999

EFFS

EFFP

PHAS

PHAP

END

 

Here, EFFS is the efficiency in S-polarization, EFFP is in P-polarization, and PHAS and PHAP are the phase shift in the two directions, in degrees.  A positive phase shift retards the wave.  To describe a coating that gives 93% efficiency and retards the P-polarization by 30 degrees relative to the S, you would enter

 

FILM

RFILM

999

.93

.93

0

30

END

SAVE CUSTOM06

 

The last line declares the dummy coating custom number 6.
