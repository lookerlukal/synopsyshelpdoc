Wizard menu commands

 

Pupil Wizard

Opens a dialog that explains most of the options that apply to the definition of the object, stop, and paraxial and real pupils.  Click OK to enter your selection into the lens.

 

Spectrum Wizard

This dialog shows the spectral curves for a variety of sources and detectors. You can combine them and then transfer the total weighted curve into the wavelength and weight arrays.

 

Edge-definition Wizard

Opens the PAD display along with the Edge-definition dialog, where you can edit edge parameters such as the edge bevel, face dimension etc.
