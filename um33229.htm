3.3.2.2.9 General-purpose Cylinder                                Previous Next TOC

 

Provision is made for adding a weak cylinder to any of the other aspheric shapes.  This is primarily so the tolerance program BTOL can model the figure error and include it in the overall budget.  Otherwise, most of the more exotic shapes, such as the USS types, are ignored by BTOL.

 

To do so, you must enter, in the BTOL input, a declaration that activates this option on a given surface, such as

 

BTOL ...

...

PFTEST sn sn ...

 

or

 

RANGE sn PFTEST dmax.

 

See the discussion in BTOL regarding this feature.

 

The attribute may also be added to an aspheric surface in an RLE or CHG file, with the input

 

sn GPCYLINDER value.
