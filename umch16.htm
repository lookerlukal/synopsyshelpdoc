16.0 THIN-FILM PROGRAM

 

SYNOPSYS contains a thin-film program for the design and analysis of multilayer coatings.

 

To access this feature, enter

 

FILM

 

at the SYNOPSYS> prompt.  The program has three basic modes: common, design, and analysis mode.

 

After a film stack has been defined as outlined in this chapter, it may be assigned to a lens surface.  Many of the features of SYNOPSYS will then include the effects of the coating on the lens performance.  See Section 17.2 for more information.
