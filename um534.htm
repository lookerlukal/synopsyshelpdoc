5.34 Glass Transmission (GTRANS)                Previous Next TOC

 

The command

 

GTRANS SN WLOW WHIGH

 

will calculate the transmission of the glass assigned to surface SN over the wavelength range from WLOW to WHIGH.  The glass must come from one of the glass tables, and it must be one for which transmission data are available.  (Some glasses lack published data, and no data at all are available for the Hoya and LZOS catalogs.)  This command may also be accessed via the MTR dialog.

 

Another feature will show the transmission curve for a selected glass type in graphic form.  This is found under the PAD GlassTable feature.  Yet another feature will show the transmission along the path of a ray through the entire lens.  This is RTRANS.
