9.4.1 Prompted DIFF input                                        Next Previous TOC

 

You may simply enter the command

 

DIFF

 

after having prepared either the COE or DPF files, described above, to activate the prompted form of DIFF.  The program will prompt for all of the input data otherwise required on the DIFF command.

 

 

9.4.2 Examples of DIFF input

 

1. A plot similar to PSPRD output (see 8.6.2) is desired, but the region to be examined is 10 Airy disk diameters.  The lens has a central obscuration of 80% of the pupil diameter.  The image is to be at HBAR = 0.10, and the peak height on the plot is to be 3 inches. (This example uses the wavefront coefficients rather than exact rays.)

 

OBS .8

COE 2.10

 

DIFF 10 50 100 3 30

 

 

 

Note that to prevent aliasing error, NPUP should be at least twice REGION.
