Apodization Dialog

 

Lenses may be apodized by entering suitable values for the quantities A, B, and C, identified on the diagram.  This will cause SYNOPSYS to reduce the intensity of each ray that intercepts that surface in accordance with the transmission function chosen.  Most of the image analysis features will honor this option, except for geometrical options such as the spot diagram, which cannot indicate the intensity of a ray.

 

If the object choice OBG is selected, the lens will automatically be apodized with a Gaussian-profile intensity distribution, and you do not have to enter any apodization data yourself.  The far-field diffraction pattern of a perfect image will then also be Gaussian in shape, as expected, taking into account the clipping of the aperture specified with the OBG data.

 

To get here: type SPS to go to the spreadsheet editor, and then click on the aperture flag of that surface.  Then click ‘Apodization’.
