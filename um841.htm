8.4.1 Footprints with other drawing programs                 Next Previous TOC

 

The rayset shown by the perspective drawing programs PER, RPER, and SOLID may be defined by the PUP and TRACE input used for footprints, if these data are included with the input for those programs, as explained in section 7.2.  Those programs can also be used to generate footprints; to do so, only the desired surface should be requested.  This feature lets you draw a footprint on any surface as seen from any angle.
