3.9.1.6 Schmidt prism (SCHMIDT)                        Previous Next TOC

 

The Schmidt prism contains a roof surface, which puts the system in nonsequential mode (see Chapter 18).

 

Number of faces: 6

Z-axis after prism: normal

Total glass path: 3.15002*WIDTH

 

RLE input:

 

SN PRISM SCHMIDT WIDTH
