15.2.1.1 Question Verbs                                                        Previous Next TOC

 

Doing any Ai task requires typing an English sentence, using normal grammar rules.  Let us first consider operation 1, evaluating a parameter.  Briefly summarized, a sentence for this purpose may consist of the following parts (although the order may in general be interchanged):

 

(conditions) (question verb) (subject) (conditions)

 

At full field find the OPD for YEN = .5

4 TH?

 

The (question verb) portion may consist of a word or phrase like

 

What is (are)

Tell me

I want to know

Find

Get

Print

Show

?

 

and the (subject) portion is a noun or phrase related to the lens data, ray data, or first-, third-, or fifth-order properties.  The AI sentence

 

Show subjects

 

will give a list of the current vocabulary.  Some of these are discussed below.
