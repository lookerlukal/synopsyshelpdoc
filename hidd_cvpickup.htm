CV Pickup Dialog

 

Use this dialog to control how the shape of this surface depends on that of another.  If a given surface is encountered more than once by a ray, it must be entered as a new surface at the appropriate location (except in nonsequential mode), and the second occurrence of the surface can be given the same shape as the first by means of a pickup declaration.  Likewise, an element can be made symmetric by placing a pickup on the second side, with the surface number to pick up given as that of the first side, with a minus sign.  You can also enter numbers for the parameters M and B, which will multiply by and add to the picked-up radius.

 

This dialog is reached via the SPS dialog.
