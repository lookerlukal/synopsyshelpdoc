5.15 Time and Date (TIME)                        Next Previous TOC

 

Most of the commands in Chapters 4 through 6 print the date.  The current time and date will also be printed in response to the command

 

TIME

 

or the AI question “DATE?”.

 

The TIME command will also print the elapsed time, in seconds, since the previous use of this command.

 

To see the elapsed time of each optimization iteration, use the command SUMMARY.
