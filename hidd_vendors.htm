Vendor Stock Lens Element Dialog

 

This dialog accesses the eight stock-lens vendor catalogs that are available in SYNOPSYS.  It is operated as follows:

 

1. Select a supplier by clicking on the desired radio button.

 

2. Click on one of the operation buttons.  For example, if you clicked on the first side of a lens element in the SketchPAD display, you have the option of matching that element to the selected catalog.  Or you can select the option to match the next lens that you click on.  You can also enter target data to match with the bottom button, or show the entire catalog of that vendor.

 

3. In all of these cases, the program will attempt to match the requested lens or data, and will then show any stock lenses that match.  The number that it shows can be adjusted as described in the User’s Manual.  When you click on one of these catalog numbers, that lens is then shown in a separate drawing window.  You can choose whether to draw the lens at a fixed scale, or adjusted to coincide with the scale of the PAD display.

 

4.  Once you have selected one of the stock lenses, you have the choice of replacing the currently selected lens element in PAD with that element (this is the lens that starts at the surface currently in the WorkSheet edit box), replacing the next lens that you click on, or inserting the stock lens at the next point where you click in an airspace.
