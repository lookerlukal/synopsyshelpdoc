Edit Glass Table Selection Dialog

 

This dialog lets you select from one of seven different catalogs of optical materials.  Choose the catalog you want, and type the glass name into the data box.  That material will then be inserted into the lens.  Or you can click on the button "Show Catalog" to bring up a display of the entire contents of the selected glass table, where you may select a glass with a double-click.

 

This dialog is reached via the SPS dialog.

 

Another way to select a glass vendor and insert a glass into a lens is via PAD | .
