4.0 LENS DATA OUTPUT

 

The specifications of the current lens may be displayed in several formats, most of which may be found in the MLL dialog.  The commands are as follows:

 

SPEC

PRT

LEO

POP

OUT

ASY

TDC

SSD

SPC

RIN

DSPEC
