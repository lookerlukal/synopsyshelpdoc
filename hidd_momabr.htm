MOM – Merit Function Definition: Raygrid-Based Aberrations

 

In this dialog you can select from a set of custom ray grids to be controlled during optimization.  These allow more flexibility in your selection than the standard raygrid option – but you must do a little more work.  Click on a ‘USE’ button, and then select the grid pattern, color, weight, and field point that you want.  You may enter the latter into the boxes labeled HBAR and GBAR, or just click on the Field Point diagram at the desired location.

 

If you select ‘Transverse aberration’, SYNOPSYS will correct the X and Y intercepts of each ray relative to that of the chief ray in the primary color.  The selection ‘OPD’ will target the optical path difference between that ray and the chief ray, and ‘Wavefront variance’ will minimize the variance of the OPDs of all of the rays in this pattern.

 

There are other options that you can put into your optimization MACro.  See section 10.3 of the SYNOPSYS User’s Manual.
