MUT – Utilities Dialog

 

The MUT dialog lists several utilities for performing manipulations or analysis of a lens.

 

Click                To

EDGE

See a table of edge thicknesses.  Feathered edges are shown with a negative thickness.

 

REVERSE

Reverse the entire lens end for end.  To reverse only a portion of the lens, use the FLIP command.

 

FEATHER

Calculate the feathering point of this surface with the next.

 

SAG

Show a table of the sag (Z-coordinate from the vertex) of the surface at several zones.

 

WEIGHT

Calculate the weight of a lens element.  To get the weight of a group of elements, use the GWGT command.

 

FLUX

Calculate the flux uniformity along a fan of rays.

 

SCALE

Scale the lens in a variety of ways.
