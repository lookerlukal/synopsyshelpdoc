5.22 Flipping a Range of Surfaces (FLIP)        Next Previous TOC

 

Often it is useful to flip a range of surfaces in a lens system.  A simple way to do this is with the FLIP command.

 

FLIP JSSS [ JSPS]

FLIP ELEMENT NB [ NB ]

 

The object and image as well as all surfaces outside of the range are left unchanged. Tilts and decenters are flipped, but options are removed from the group.

 

You can FLIP a range of surfaces in a ZFILE zoom lens, as long as the range includes none of the zoom groups, or else includes a single, entire, group.

 

The REVERSE command will reverse the entire lens.

 

The WorkSheet program can also flip an element, with immediate graphical feedback.
