Birefringent Materials Dialog

 

A birefringent material may be specified in two ways:

 

1. The name ‘CALCITE’,'CRQUARTZ', or 'SAPBIREF' can be entered in the Name box, and the radio button ‘Biref from database’ selected.

 

2. The radio button ‘Special’ can be selected and the ordinary and extraordinary indices entered into the data boxes.

 

In either case, the crystal axis direction must be entered in the direction cosine boxes.

 

Caution: Avoid orienting the axis so that a ray propagates exactly in that direction; this leads to a singularity because the index is not defined in that direction.

 

This dialog is reached via the SPS dialog or from the WoekSheet..
