7.6 Plot Utilities (saving, EPL)                Next Previous TOC

 

All of the plotted output in SYNOPSYS except for the split-screen PAD display and the RPER display may be stored and retrieved later.  (And the PAD display format may be saved with the SSU command.)  The simplest way to do this is to click on the buttons and on the toolbar at the top of the Graphics Window.  This will save the picture, along with any annotation that you may have added, in a file with the extension SSP.

 

The command EPL may be used in rare cases where a plot was not completed normally and is not yet shown on the screen.  This command flushes the contents of the plot buffer, allowing you to make a new picture.
