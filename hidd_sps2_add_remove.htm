Add and Remove Dialog

 

This dialog lets you alter the number of surfaces in your lens.

 

To truncate a lens at a desired surface, click Make target the highest surface number, enter the desired number in the Target surface box, and click OK.

 

You can also add or delete surfaces before a target surface by selecting the Add or Delete buttons, and entering the target surface number and clicking OK.

 

Most simple insertion or deletion tasks are more easily done with the buttons , , and .  Those buttons will insert a surface at the current line, delete the current surface, or truncate the lens at that surface, deleting all higher-numbered surfaces. If you click in a line below the last surface in the lens and then click the button, all surfaces between the lens and where you clicked will be added.

 

A more graphical way to add or remove surfaces is with the WorkSheet, which has the above as well as several more kinds of buttons.
