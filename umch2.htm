Chapter 2

 

2.0 BASIC CONCEPTS

 

In the last chapter you saw some of the ways in which a user interacts with SYNOPSYS.  Many of the command-mode functions described in this manual may also be obtained in AI with completely different input; in some cases the AI input is simpler, and sometimes the reverse is true.  You may select the most convenient.  You can also access many features via a dialog tree, which starts with the dialog MMM (Main Menu Mode), and where the dialog MLI (Menu List) lists the entire tree.  Each dialog also has its own mnemonic, and once you learn it you don’t have to navigate the tree at all.
