Now we create another MACro that will utilize this database.  It starts with the lens at location number 9 in the database, and then alters it so as to give an afocal magnification of 0.2, and a total length of about 300 mm.  We do not require color correction in this example.

 

; XSYS.MAC

; HERE IS A SIMPLE EXAMPLE OF HOW THE EXPERT-SYSTEMS PROGRAM

; XSYS CAN HELP YOU FIND STARTING POINTS.  WE OPEN A PRELIMINARY

; LENS (USE 9) FROM THE XSYS DATABASE, AND THEN ASK IT

; TO FIND COMBINATIONS WHERE THE AFOCAL MAGNIFICATION IS 0.2

; WITH A WEIGHT OF 10

 

XSYS

USE 9

XSYS

ALTER

AFMAG .2 10

TOTL 300 .01

CWT 0

PASSES 5

PLOT AUTO

snap

GO
