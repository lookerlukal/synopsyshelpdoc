6.5 First-Order Analysis (FIRST)                                Next Previous TOC

 

The first-order properties of a lens or a portion of a lens may be analyzed with the command

 

FIRST [ JSSS [ JSPS ]. ]

 

where JSSS and JSPS are the first and last surfaces in the lens to be included in the analysis.  Default is the whole lens, and if you enter but a single surface number, you get an analysis of just that surface.  A sample listing is shown below. FIRST may be run interactively from the dialog MFT.

 

SYNOPSYS AI>first
