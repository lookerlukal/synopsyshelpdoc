4.8 List NURBS Control points (LNC)                        Previous Next TOC

 

The command

 

LNC isn

 

will list the definition of a NURBS surface declared with USS types 15 or 24.  A table is produced showing the (X,Y,Z) coordinates of each control point and the sag at that point.  See Section 3.3.2.7 for details about this surface type.  The lens below has been assigned a NURBS type 24 at surface 5.  The LNC command produces a list of the control points and the surface sag at each one.  Note that the surface does not actually to through any of the control points except at the corners.
