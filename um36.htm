3.6 Changing a lens                                        Previous Next TOC

 

After a lens has been input to SYNOPSYS by either an RLE file or a GET or FETCH command, the specifications may be altered in a number of ways, the most basic of which is with a CHG file, described below.  Other ways include the AI program (see Chapter 15), the LE lens editor (see 3.6.2), the SYS and SPS dialogs, and the WorkSheet (see Chapter 13).
