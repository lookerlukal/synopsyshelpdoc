17.2.5 Mirror materials                                                        Next Previous TOC

 

For convenience, this section gives the published optical constants of a variety of metals useful for mirrors.  You may select the index and extinction index K for the metal and wavelength of your system.  These data are taken from Ordal et al, Appl. Opt. 22 No. 7, pp. 1099-1119 (1983) and from data published at Filmetrics.com.  For more complete information, you are referred to those sources.

 

If your coating has a substrate of aluminum and operates in the wavelength region from 0.18 um to 20 um, you should enter the word ALUMINUM in word four of the stack definition on the substrate line.  The program will automatically recalculate N and K for the current wavelengths.  The table below gives samples of the data so calculated.
