17.2.7 Surface coating output                                                Next Previous TOC

 

The command-level mnemonics

 

PCOAT                 (Print COATings)

PCOAT SN

PCOAT ALL

 

may be entered to examine the coatings currently declared.  The first gives a summary table of the designations.  The second will print the stack data used by the program when evaluating the efficiency of the coating on the given surface; this is useful to check whether the coating is as desired, was properly reversed following glass, etc.  The third form will print the stack data for the entire lens.

 

The AI program can also print coating data, in response to a question like "Coating of surface 4?".
