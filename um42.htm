4.2 Print Data (PRT)                                Previous Next TOC

 

The command

 

 

prints the lens data in floating-point format.  This output contains more significant digits than SPEC, and also lists the exact indices at all wavelengths.  The optional [SYS] gives just system information (object code, APS, etc.), and [SURF] just the surface data.  If a surface number is entered, only a single line giving the data for that surface is printed, and if two surface numbers are given with JSSS, JSPS, that portion of the lens is listed.  A surface with a TAG (see 3.3.12) is indicated with a "T", and data for a tagged surface will be output even if a different surface number or [SYS] is entered.  A PRT listing is automatically followed by an ASY or ASY GLOBAL (if the optional GLOBAL is entered) listing (see below) if tilts, decenters, or deformations are present in the lens, and if the lens is in EXTERNAL mode and EXTERNAL is entered, the output will include an ASY EXTERNAL.  A sample PRT listing is shown below.  This feature is also found in the MLL dialog.

 

You can defeat the listing of the surface flags (“S”, “P”, etc.) by turning on switch 74.
