The output from this input sequence is given below.  Some items should be noticed: surface 4 was assigned custom coating number 1, which is a three-layer stack.  The listing from the command PCOA 4 shows that the incident medium for the stack has an index of 1.8236, which is the index of that element, and the stack is inverted, since surface number 4 has a glass-air interface.  The PRAY commands show that essentially no energy gets through with the on-axis ray but for the quadrant ray the amplitude is greater.  Even though the polarizer is crossed with respect to the object, the polarization is rotated slightly at lens surfaces in the quadrants (but not in the meridional or skew planes), and some energy passes through the polarizer.  The AI example shows another evaluation of the transmission for that ray. AI could also plot the transmission as a function of anything that Al can recognize, or anything that can be changed in a MACro.
