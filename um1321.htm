13.21 The Format Coach                                                Previous Next TOC

 

This handy help feature can help jog your memory when you are creating a MACro but are not sure of the file or data formats.  Just click on the button when the editor is open and you get a dialog that shows you the format of RLE files and optimization files.  This dialog displays numerous examples of those files, and sometimes seeing an example is all you need.

 

In the event that you need more complete information, just click the Help button on the Coach dialog, and you go immediately to the section of the User’s Manual that covers that topic.
