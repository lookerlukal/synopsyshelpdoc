8.5.14 Lateral Color Plot (LCPLOT)                                         Next Previous TOC

 

A convenient way to analyze the lateral color aberration of your lens is with the command

 

LCPLOT [ NB [ SCF ] ]

 

NB is the number of field points to analyze, with a default of 10 and a maximum of 50, and SCF is the aberration value per one inch on the plot.  This feature may be run from the MRR dialog.

 

Here is an examle:

 

GET 1

LCPLOT

 

 

SYNOPSYS AI>GET 1
