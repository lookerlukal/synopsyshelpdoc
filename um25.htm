2.5 Sign conventions                                                        Previous Next TOC

 

The fundamental convention in SYNOPSYS is that light must intercept each surface in sequence, and cannot skip a surface or back up (except for some of the ghost image programs).  Lenses following this convention are said to be in SEQUENTIAL mode, and obey a number of rules, given below.  A separate set of conventions and rules, applying to NONSEQUENTIAL mode, is described in Chapter 18.
