2.4.2 Analysis in other coordinate systems                                Previous Next TOC

 

The above paragraphs refer to the input of surface positions.  SYNOPSYS also can evaluate and display the location of each surface in the coordinate system of any selected surface (see section 4.6) or even in the coordinate system of an external reference (see section 3.8.4).  It can give the path of a ray through the lens in any desired coordinate system (see 6.3.2), and can target the global coordinates of surfaces and rays in the aberration definition file (see 10.3.1.2 and 10.3.3).
