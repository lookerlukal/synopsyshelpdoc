15.3.5 Alternate starting points                                                Previous Next TOC

 

The process of finding alternate starting points involves three phases: first, potential matches are rated and sorted by quality.  Second, you are prompted to select one of the ten highest matches, which is then displayed on the screen (if SNAP is requested) and placed on the XSYS plot (if PLOT is requested).  Third, you are queried whether a quick optimization should be performed on the currently selected match.  At any point, you may exit from XSYS (with the current lens or a selected match) and resume normal processing.  If PLOT AUTO is requested, XSYS will automatically select all 10 matches in sequence for the XSYS plot.
