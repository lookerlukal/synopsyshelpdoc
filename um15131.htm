15.1.3.1 Passing values with the AlParameter                                Previous Next TOC

 

Section 15.1.2.2 described how to use the AIParameter in an implied loop.  This symbol can also be used as a temporary vehicle to pass the value of one quantity to another.  With the special verb AIPSET, any quantity that could be found or evaluated by normal Al sentences may be formatted and placed into the AIP symbol.  This symbol then may be used as any other symbol.

 

AIPSET YA on surface 6 at full field

CHG

6 RAO .5 .8 0 AIP

END

 

In this example the Y-coordinate of the full-field chief ray on surface 6 is formatted as a floating-point number and placed into the AIP symbol (by the AIPSET sentence).  The CHG file then uses this value as the Y coordinate of the decenter of a rectangular aperture on that surface.  If you entered SHOW SYMBOLS at this point, the symbol AIP would be listed with a numeric string, equal to the ray coordinate.  Also permitted is the format AIPSET = ….

 

You can also use the Zn parameters for a similar purpose.
