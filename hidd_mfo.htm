MID – Image Model Analysis

 

When an image model has been prepared.  The MID (Menu, Image Dissection) dialog will perform a variety of kinds of analysis of that model.  These will superimpose a detector of a given size, shape, and position on the image, and then determine the amount of energy that gets through as a function of either the size or position of that detector.  This feature is most often used to get an encircled energy plot, slit trace, or knife-edge trace (but see KNI for a purely geometric version, or type MGI).  See FOR for the command-mode form of this feature.

 

A command mode form, with additional features, is also available.  See DETECTOR.
